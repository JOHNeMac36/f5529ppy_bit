How to play

Gameplay

You have to defend Earth from invading hexadecimal aliens. Each alien has a value ranging from 00 to ff and the only way to kill them is to shoot them with a photon laser that targets all aliens with a specific value. Your goal is to quickly set your laser's bits to the binary values of the invading aliens to prevent them from landing. If an alien gets passed your defense, then its GAME OVER!

Controls

Use the 1-8 keys on the keyboard to flip bits

Scoring

You get 1 point for every kill.
